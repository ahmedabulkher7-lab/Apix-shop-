SHIPPING & RETURNS

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Standard free shipping within the continental United States takes 3-5 business days. Expedited shipping options (1-2 days) are available at checkout for an additional fee.

Currently, we ship to the United States and Canada. We are working on expanding our logistics to support worldwide shipping by late 2026.

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you are not completely satisfied with your APEX equipment, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund (minus return shipping costs). The item must be in its original condition and packaging.

WARRANTY

Our limited lifetime warranty covers structural defects in all steel components (e.g., welds breaking, bars bending under normal use within weight limits). It does not cover cosmetic wear and tear, such as scratches to the powder coating from carabiners or rings, or rust due to outdoor exposure.
